Lemlist Workstream Guide

AI-powered LinkedIn automation for sales prospecting and outreach optimization.

What is Lemlist?

Lemlist is an AI-powered LinkedIn automation platform designed specifically for sales teams. It enables Business Development Representatives (BDRs) to scale outbound prospecting with personalized, multi-touch campaigns while maintaining high engagement rates. The platform combines intelligent lead enrichment, automated sequencing, and campaign analytics to help teams generate more qualified opportunities with less manual effort.

For EasyVista, Lemlist will streamline our BDR team's outreach process, enabling them to efficiently manage larger prospect lists while delivering personalized messaging that resonates with targets.

Best Practices for BDR Teams

Campaign Excellence

1. Personalization Over Volume

Always customize the first message with specific details about the prospect's company, role, or recent news. Generic templates underperform even with AI optimization.

2. Multi-Touch Sequences

Use 5-7 step sequences with varied content types (LinkedIn, email, social content). This increases response rates while respecting preference and unsubscribe patterns.

3. Segment Your Lists

Create separate campaigns for different prospect personas, industries, and stages. This allows for more targeted messaging and better performance tracking.

4. Weekly Optimization

Review analytics every week. Identify top-performing sequences and apply learnings immediately. Test subject lines, call-to-actions, and timing continuously.

5. Hand-Off to Sales

Move warm leads to sales immediately. Don't hold prospects in sequences too long. Track hand-off quality through conversion rates to refine targeting.

6. Respect LinkedIn Limits

Stay within LinkedIn's daily connection (50) and message (100) limits to avoid account restrictions. Pace campaigns accordingly across multiple users.

7. Monitor Engagement Signals

Pay attention to message views, profile visits, and profile clicks. These indicate genuine interest even if there's no direct response yet.

8. Maintain CRM Discipline

Ensure every Lemlist action syncs to CRM. Use standardized lead scoring so sales knows which prospects are warm.

Getting Started with Lemlist

Quick-Start Guide

1
Create Your Lemlist Account

Sign up with your EasyVista email. Complete LinkedIn authentication during onboarding. Verify that your LinkedIn profile meets Lemlist's requirements (at least 500 connections recommended).

2
Connect Your CRM (Salesforce)

Link Lemlist to Salesforce via OAuth. Map required fields (contact name, company, industry, etc.). Test sync to ensure data flows correctly before campaign launch.

3
Import Your First Prospect List

Start with 100-200 prospects from a single vertical or company size. Validate data quality (correct roles, active companies). Lemlist will enrich with additional firmographic data.

4
Build Your First Campaign

Name the campaign clearly (e.g., "US Mid-Market Tech Directors Q1"). Choose campaign type (LinkedIn only, LinkedIn + Email, or outbound channel mix).

5
Create Sequence Steps

Step 1 (LinkedIn connection): Personal note (40-50 chars). Step 2 (1 day wait): Follow-up message if no action. Step 3 (3 days wait): Value-add content or question. Continue through Step 5-7 with varied approaches.

6
Personalization Setup

Use dynamic fields like {{first_name}}, {{company}}, {{recent_news}}. Insert 1-2 specific references per message. AI will suggest personalization improvements.

7
Launch and Monitor

Start with 50 prospects for day 1 (test run). Monitor for the first 48 hours. Check response quality and adjust as needed before scaling to full list.

8
Analyze and Optimize

Review analytics dashboard daily first week, then weekly. Track: open rates (target 30%+), reply rates (target 5%+), and conversion to opportunity (target 1-2% of sent).

Pro Tip: Save time by using Lemlist's AI writing assistant to draft sequences. Always review and personalize before sending. The tool provides suggestions, but human judgment on relevance is critical.
